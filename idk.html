Tourist spots in Idukki

Eravikulam Wildlife Sanctuary
Eravikulam is wildlife sanctuary proclaimed as a National Park. It supports the largest population of Nilgiri tahr in the world. Anamudi peak is on the southern part of this park. The famous Neelakurinji grows here. It has a flowering cycle of 12 years.

Idukki Dam
The Idukki Dam is a double curvature Arch dam constructed across the Periyar River in a narrow gorge between two granite hills locally known as Kuravan and Kurathi in Kerala, India. At 168.91 metres, it is one of the highest arch dams in Asia.
